# Privacy Policy for AI Research Paper Helper

**Last updated: February 2024**

## Overview

AI Research Paper Helper is a browser extension designed to help users understand research papers. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

- **Page content**: When you use the extension on a research paper, we extract text content from the page for analysis
- **User settings**: Your preferences (API mode, backend URL) are stored locally in your browser

### What We Don't Collect

- Personal information
- Browsing history
- Cookies or tracking data
- Data from pages you don't actively analyze

## Data Processing

### Local Processing
- Content extraction happens entirely in your browser
- User settings are stored locally using Chrome's storage API

### Backend Processing
- When you request summaries, Q&A, or equation explanations, the extracted text is sent to our backend server
- The backend processes the request and returns results
- **We do not store your documents or queries permanently**

## Third-Party Services

The extension may use the following third-party services for AI processing:
- **Groq API** - For fast LLM inference
- **OpenRouter** - For access to various AI models

These services have their own privacy policies.

## Data Security

- All API communications use HTTPS encryption
- No user data is logged or stored on our servers beyond temporary processing
- Paper content is processed in memory and discarded after the response is sent

## Your Rights

You can:
- Clear all extension data via Chrome settings
- Disable the extension at any time
- Use "Local Only" mode to avoid sending data to external servers

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

---

**Summary**: We only process the content you explicitly ask us to analyze. We don't track you, store your documents, or collect personal information.
